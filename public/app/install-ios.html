Why can't I just get it from the App Store?

Apple requires app developers to sign up for their Apple Developer program in order to release apps onto the app store, and doing so requires an annual payment of $99. However, Remchi is completely free and I make no money off of it, which means I don't have a source of income to fund that. And, because Remchi is a PWA, it can already be directly "installed" with this method (which is intentional by Apple). Because of that, it's simply not worth it to spend such money trying to get the app officially onto the App Store.

tl;dr Ryan's broke and can't afford paying Apple $99/year
